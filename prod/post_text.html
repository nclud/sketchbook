Sketchbook Sessions: A Creative Speaker Series

February 26, 2014

We have a rich history of knowledge sharing at nclud – our company is built on the value of fostering community. Our closest colleagues & clients got to know us by spending time in our studio and talking with us. Delivering great content & events has been such a rewarding part of our culture that we’re giving it a name and clearer purpose: Sketchbook Sessions.

Sketchbook Sessions will continue our tradition of producing free & open talks that have included speakers like Aaron Draplin, Ian MacKaye, Jon Newman, El Jefe, & others – yet also hosting inspiring discussions & in-depth presentations by our team members.

As we further the conversation on the convergence of design, technology, and creativity we are taking Sketchbook Sessions national with partners like General Assembly, HootSuite, and more to be announced. Furthermore, sponsorship of these events will fit our model of giving back directly to the community.

In the meantime, I’d like to invite you to the first installment in this series tonight, on the Art & Code of Data Visualization with Scott Murray co-presented by Data Visualization DC. We’re really excited to present this platform that goes behind the scenes of our company, community, & industry to create an open & creative discourse.

Joe Corbett Joe is the COO at nclud, and focuses on providing the team with the best systems and tools in the business to ensure we consistantly deliver the leading creative solutions in our industry.
